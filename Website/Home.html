AIR POLLUTION ANALYSIS


INTRODUCTION

Air pollution is a growing concern worldwide, affecting not only the quality of the environment but also the health of living organisms. The introduction of harmful substances into the Earth's atmosphere has led to significant consequences, from adverse health effects in humans to ecological damage.

Poor air quality can have far-reaching consequences, affecting both human and environmental well-being. Air pollution not only damages crops, animals, and forests but also contaminates bodies of water, endangering aquatic ecosystems.

In humans, it leads to a range of health issues, particularly respiratory and cardiovascular diseases, which pose a significant public health challenge. This project aims to address the critical issue of air pollution, with a focus on five different states in India during the years 2008-2012.
